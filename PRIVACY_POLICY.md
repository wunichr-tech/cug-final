# CUG Wellbeing — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 2026
**Operated by:** Central University Ghana, Accra, Ghana

## What we collect
- **Account details:** your name, university email address, student/staff ID, and role.
- **Wellbeing check-ins:** your daily motivation score (1–5), the factor tags you select, and any optional note you write.

## How we use it
- To show you your own wellbeing history, streaks, and averages.
- To help authorised university staff identify students who may need support, so they can reach out early.
- To display campus updates relevant to you.

## Who can see your data
- **You** can always see your own check-ins.
- **Only verified, approved lecturers and student-support staff** can view student check-in data. This restriction is enforced at the database level, and staff access requires approval by the Dean's Office.
- Your optional check-in notes are visible to staff **only if you tap "Flag for support"**; otherwise they remain private to you.
- We never sell your data or share it with third parties outside the university.

## Where your data lives
Data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest, in a European data centre region.

## Your rights
You may request a copy of your data or full deletion of your account and all associated check-ins at any time by contacting the Student Affairs Office or emailing wellbeing@cug.edu.gh. Deletion requests are completed within 30 days.

## Crisis situations
If a check-in indicates an immediate risk of harm, university counselling staff may contact you directly as part of their duty of care.

## Changes
We will notify users in the app of any material changes to this policy.
